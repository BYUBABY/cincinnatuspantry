About Me

My name is Cincinnatus and this is my pantry. Music is my dearest hobby (I can't quite justify making it my career). In this pantry of mine you can get a taste of what kind of music is part of my lifestyle. They have different values in my mind, but I couldn't imagine my life without any of these selections.

My Musical Experience

Ever since I was 4, I've been playing the piano. I was never clasically trained with the instrument, but that doesn't make me any less profficient. The piano is the instrument that I am most comfortable playing. Whenever I have a musical idea pop in my head or whenever I have a yearning to play a nostalgic piece, the piano is what I turn to.

Along with the piano comes the Organ. I was introduced to the Organ at first through the Synth. I like to call the Organ the Synth's Father. I've recently been formally tutored on organ performance. I'm not as comfortable as I am with the Piano (or the synth for that matter), but I have enjoyed my experience with it thus far.

The Trombone is such a fun instrument that I played through all of my teenage years. It introduced me to some of my most prized genres of music: Classical and Jazz. There isn't anything like playing a Trombone especially with a good combo to back you up.

The Drumset and any other Percussive have a rhythmic power to them that cannot be created by any other artificial means. I learned to play the percussive instruments along with the trombone in my teenage years. This training has been the most valuable for me as I am now very profficient in musical rhythm.
